Vision Labs is pioneering the next generation of dermatological diagnostics through advanced Artificial Intelligence.

The Vision Labs Mission

Empowering early detection for a healthier tomorrow.

Based in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, India, Vision Labs was founded by a team of dedicated Computer Science Engineers. Our mission is to democratize high-end medical diagnostics by making them accessible, affordable, and instant through the power of Deep Learning.

Our flagship project, DermAI, focuses on the early detection of critical skin conditions, ensuring that distance or lack of resources never stands in the way of professional health insights.

The Technical Backbone

Our platform is built using state-of-the-art architectures to ensure clinical-grade accuracy.

EfficientNet-B0

Utilizing the EfficientNet architecture, our model provides an optimal balance between parameter efficiency and high-resolution classification accuracy for skin lesion analysis.

Multi-Class Logic

Our system is specifically optimized to distinguish between Acne, Eczema, and Pigmentation with high sensitivity and recall.

Data Privacy
